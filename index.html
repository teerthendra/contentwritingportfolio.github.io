Sample Writings

How AI is Changing Our Daily Lives

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer a sci-fi fantasy – it's here, simplifying our lives day by day. From Siri reminding you to set reminders to Netflix recommending your next binge-watching show, AI surrounds us. In Uttar Pradesh, where I am from, farmers are now employing AI tools to forecast weather and enhance crops. Firms such as Google and xAI are breaking boundaries, creating smarter machines that can learn and think. Picture this – a future where AI is taking you to the office or assisting doctors in making quicker diagnoses! But not all is rosy – there are concerns that some jobs will be replaced. However, I am convinced that AI is a tool that, when used correctly, can solve massive problems and help us inch towards a world that is tech-enabled. What's your opinion – friend or enemy to AI?

Top 5 Tech Trends to Watch in 2025

Tech is progressing at a great speed, and 2025 will be one hell of an exciting year! For starters, AI is becoming smarter – think about chatbots such as Grok that virtually become human-like. Second, 5G will finally roll out to tier II and III cities in India, making internet incredibly fast. Third, wearable technology, such as smartwatches monitoring your health, is on a roll. Fourth, electric cars are expanding, with Tata in the lead in UP and beyond. Lastly, quantum computing may sound technical, but it's around the corner to crack issues we cannot even dream of today. As a native of Uttar Pradesh, I'm excited to learn how these trends will influence our future – from improved connectivity in villages to hi-tech employment. Which trend do you look forward to the most?
